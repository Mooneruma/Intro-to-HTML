Now local


Zanzibars is now local to St. Paul, Minnesota. We are new coffee house that roasts coffee from the best sources around the world. We are open from 6am to 10pm weekdays and till midnight on Fridays and Saturdays.


Live Music


We feature many live artists that play modern music selections. We offer open stage on Saturdays and have a schedule for traveling bands playing on the weekends here.



Beverages and Food


Zanzibars is the place to go when it comes to our delicious brews of coffee. To go along with your coffee we provide many selections of food from sandwiches and soups to deserts such as cakes pastries. Our menu is provided here.


Atmostphere for our location


We focus on being an urban funk "hangout" being located in the middle of the college town center. Our main focus creating an enviroment for college students and parents alike while featuring a modern theme. Come to Zanzibars and take a seat now!
